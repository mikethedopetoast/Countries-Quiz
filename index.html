How To Play

  1. Select a continent (default: World), then change the 'independent' status (default: All Countries) and then click the 'start' button to begin the quiz.
  2. Refer to the flag and relevant information of each country provided during the quiz.
  3. Go to the input field and type the answer, and then click the 'next' button to submit the answer.
  4. Check your progress at the top of the site, and continue on answering until the end to see how well you did.
